Mirawatt T5K

Mirawatt T5K - is an application for viewing power monitoring data collected by the TED-5000 home energy monitor.

A simple swipe gesture on the graph (left of right) will change the view from Hour, Day, to Week etc...

You need to have the TED-5000 device installed and functioning for this application to be useful.

The TED-5000 gateway also needs to be network accessible from your iPhone/iTouch; this typically means using the app inside your home network.

For more information on the TED device see the manufacturer's site at: http://www.theenergydetective.com
