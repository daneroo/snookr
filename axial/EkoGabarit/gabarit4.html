The Sustainable Architecture & Building Awards recognizes and awards excellence in the design and execution of Canadian buildings of all types, renovations and interiors.
Fill in the registration form, without obligation at www.sabmagazine.com, Awards section, or call 1-800-520-6281, x304. Selected projects will be publicized in the media, in a special awards edition of SABMag, on the SABMag web site, and in speaking engagements.
A jury of distinguished professionnals will adjudicate the awards based on criteria of sustainable design, architectural excellence and technical innovation.
Deadline for submission, April 24, 2009.
